Community Banking
We are a grassroots organized cooperative that has been running since February of 2013.  for women living in abject poverty on the Haitian/Dominican border (Jimaní). We work to create a better future for ourselves and our families. We create value and wealth through the exchange of importnat information including market analysis.  by offering them access to culturally and linguistically sensitive programs that focus on business development and financial savings.Additionally, we support them as they work to secure the documentation that they need to legally work in the Dominican Republic.  EndFragment
